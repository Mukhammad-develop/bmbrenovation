Victorian terraces are a significant part of the Harrow housing stock, and they do have specific characteristics worth knowing about. Bathrooms in these properties were typically retrofitted at a later date — often carved out of a bedroom — which means pipe routing can be non-standard, cast iron soil stacks may have been extended and adapted multiple times, floors may not be perfectly level, and walls may not be square. None of this is a problem for our team — we have fitted bathrooms in dozens of Victorian terraces across North West London and Hertfordshire. We check all of this during the free home visit and factor any adjustments into the written quote upfront, so there are no surprises once work begins.
