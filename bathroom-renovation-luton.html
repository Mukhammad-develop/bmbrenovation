Yes — damp in bathrooms is very common, particularly in older Luton properties where waterproofing was never properly done or has failed over time. We identify the source of the damp, treat it correctly, and install proper cement board backing and tanking before tiling. Painting over damp or sealing it in is not something we do — it comes back every time.

Yes — bath-to-shower conversions are one of the most requested jobs we do. Many Luton properties have bathrooms where the bath takes up most of the available floor space and rarely gets used. Yes — flats are common across Luton and we work in them regularly. We're considerate of shared access, working hours, and noise in apartment blocks.
